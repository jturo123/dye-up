Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

The short version: Dye Up does not collect any data about you.

No accounts, no tracking, no analytics, no advertising, and nothing sent to a server.

Information we collect

None. Dye Up does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. The app has no user accounts, no analytics or tracking software, no advertising networks, and it does not connect to any server operated by us.

Information stored on your device

Dye Up saves your game settings and progress locally on your device so the app remembers them between sessions. This includes:

  • Your selected board, background, die, and trail
  • Which items you have unlocked or purchased
  • Your adventure progress
  • Sound and music preferences
  • Whether you have completed the tutorial

This information never leaves your device. It is not visible to us and is not shared with anyone. Deleting the app removes it.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or handle your payment information. Apple provides the app with a receipt confirming what you own, so the items you bought can be unlocked and restored. Apple's handling of your data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's privacy

Because Dye Up collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from any user, regardless of age.

Third parties

Dye Up includes no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with third parties, because no data is collected.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date at the top. If a future version of the app ever begins collecting data, this policy will be updated before that version is released.
