The popular dice game you've seen at every party.

Roll. Bounce. Catch. Sink.

Toss the die across the table, land it on your opponent's side, and score before they can catch it. Sink it straight into a cup for the big points, and watch it splash.

How to play

  • Swipe to toss. Clear the height line and land on your opponent's half.
  • They get one tap to catch it. Miss, and you score.
  • Land it in a cup for a SINK, worth 3 points.
  • Clip the rim on the way out for a TINK, worth 2 points.
  • Throw it long and you get a FIFA. Kick it up with your foot and catch it.
  • Throw it short into your own cup and it's a SELF SINK, an instant loss.

What's in it

  • 16 worlds to unlock, from a volcano to Mount Everest to a stadium under the lights
  • Five CPU difficulty levels, each with its own rival and look
  • An adventure mode with five stops to climb
  • 9 dice colors, 8 trails, custom boards, all 50 state flags, and 100 country flags
  • Two player pass-and-play on one phone
Free to play. No ads. No data collected.

Dye Up doesn't track you, doesn't phone home, and doesn't collect anything about you. Everything stays on your device.
